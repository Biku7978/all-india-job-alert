🏢 About GR Infracon Private Limited

GR Infracon Private Limited is one of the growing construction and infrastructure companies in India. The company is involved in residential, commercial and high-rise building projects with a strong focus on quality, safety and timely project completion. GR Infracon offers excellent career opportunities for experienced engineers, project managers, site supervisors and quality professionals.

The company is currently expanding its team for ongoing projects in Kollur, Hyderabad. Professionals with experience in high-rise residential construction, project planning, quality control, quantity surveying and site execution are encouraged to apply. GR Infracon provides a professional work environment where employees can improve their technical knowledge and build long-term careers in the construction industry.

🛠 Roles & Responsibilities

  • Manage day-to-day construction activities at the project site.
  • Ensure quality standards are maintained during execution.
  • Coordinate with architects, consultants and contractors.

  • Maintain safety standards and company guidelines.
  • Prepare project schedules and monitor timelines.
