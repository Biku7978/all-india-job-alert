About Vaswani Construction

Vaswani Construction is a well-established construction company known for delivering premium residential, commercial and infrastructure projects across India. The company focuses on quality construction, modern engineering practices and timely project completion. Over the years, Vaswani has built a strong reputation through its commitment to safety, innovation and customer satisfaction.

The organization offers excellent career opportunities for experienced engineers, supervisors, managers, technicians and fresh graduates. Employees receive professional training, exposure to advanced construction technologies and opportunities to work on prestigious projects with experienced industry professionals.

Why Join Vaswani Construction?

    Eligibility Criteria

    • Candidates should possess the required qualification for the applied position.
    • Relevant experience in Construction, Civil Engineering or Infrastructure projects is preferred.
    • Fresh Civil Engineering (B.E./DCE) candidates can apply for Fresher positions.
    • Good communication and teamwork skills are required.
    • Candidates should be willing to work at project locations.
    • Knowledge of safety standards and quality control will be an added advantage.

    Job Roles & Responsibilities

    Selected candidates will be responsible for project planning, execution, supervision, billing, quality control, safety management and overall site coordination depending on their respective positions. Project Managers will oversee complete project execution, manpower planning, budgeting and client coordination. Planning Engineers will prepare schedules, monitor progress and ensure timely completion of work.

    Billing Engineers will prepare RA bills, BOQs and quantity calculations. Safety Managers and Safety Supervisors will ensure compliance with workplace safety standards and conduct regular inspections. Quality Engineers and Lab Technicians will maintain construction quality and testing procedures. Engineers, Supervisors, Foremen and Operators will manage daily construction activities while ensuring productivity, quality and safety.
